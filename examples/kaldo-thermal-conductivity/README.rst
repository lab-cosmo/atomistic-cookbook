Thermal conductivity with kaldo and UPET
=========================================

This recipe demonstrates how to compute lattice thermal conductivity
using `kaldo <https://nanotheorygroup.github.io/kaldo/>`_ for
anharmonic lattice dynamics and the `UPET
<https://github.com/lab-cosmo/pet>`_ universal machine-learning
potential (PET-MAD) as the force engine. The example computes force
constants, phonon dispersions, and thermal conductivity of NaCl
(rocksalt) at 300 K.
